Hi, my name is Courtney Schild.

I’m a Digital Designer with years of experience, education and training in the fields of user research, user experience (UX) design, user interface (UI) design, and usability testing. I’m a creative problem solver with a skilled understanding of user-centered design (UCD) and a natural ability to work within constraints, connecting human needs and business goals to simplify complex problems into innovative design solutions for mobile, web and desktop applications.

With a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design and a minor in New Media Communications from Oregon State University, I excel in all that is visual communication, from brand strategy to visual design to content development. My career is comprised of freelance and in-house work for a variety of clients and employers, from smaller agencies and studios to larger corporations.

"The thing I liked best about working with Courtney is that I always felt like I could put any challenge in front of her and she’d figure it out. Not just design-wise; a new technology, a business process, or just a way to do this thing better, she’d figure it out."

Julie Bonaduce, Graphic Designer
